Privacy PolicyNight Crow For Safari

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Night Crow For Safari is built to respect your privacy completely. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or any record of your browsing.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no account, no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, and no advertising. We have no servers that your browsing touches.

How the extension works

  • To apply a dark theme, the extension reads and restyles the content of the pages you choose to allow it on. All of this happens on your device, in real time. The content of the pages you visit is never sent anywhere.
  • Your settings (theme preferences, per-site rules, schedules, custom CSS) are stored locally on your device using Safari's extension storage. They are not transmitted to us or to any third party.
  • You can disable the extension globally, or on individual sites, at any time.

Data sharing

None. Because no data is collected, there is nothing to sell, share, or hand to any third party.

Children

The extension collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
